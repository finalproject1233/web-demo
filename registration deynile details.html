Macluumaad Dheeri ah

Bedka dhulka waxaa lagu cabbiraa hector (ha) ama mitir laba jibbaaran (m²). Halkan waxaa ku yaal cabbirro caadi ah:
1. *1 Hector* = 10,000 mitir laba jibbaaran (m²)
2. *1 Acre* = 4,046.86 mitir laba jibbaaran (m²) = qiyaastii 0.4047 hector
3. *1 Mitir laba jibbaaran* = 0.0001 hector
### Tusaalooyinka:
- *Boos*: Haddii boosku leeyahay bed 0.5 hector, waxay u dhigantaa 5,000 m².
- *Nus Boos*: Haddii nus boosku leeyahay bed 0.25 hector, waxay u dhigantaa 2,500 m².
Haddii aad u baahan tahay cabbir gaar ah ama macluumaad dheeri ah, fadlan ii sheeg!
